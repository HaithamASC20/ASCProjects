Lebron James

Lebron James Gets Drafted Out of Highschool

Lebron James was drafted first overall by the Cleveland Cavaliers in 2003. This was the beginning of his amazing career.

Lebron James Gets Traded to the Miami Heat

Lebron James was traded to the Miami Heat in 2010. He did this in hopes of winning a championship through playing with Chris Bosh and Dwyane Wade to create the iconic "Big 3." This was the beginning of an era.

Lebron James Wins First NBA Championship

After joining Miami, Lebron James secured his spot as an NBA great through winning his first NBA Championship in 2012. He did this in great style and secured even the Finals MVP award.

Lebron James Return to Cavaliers

After winning 2 NBA Championships in Miami, Lebron decided to return to his home of Cleveland in 2014. Here he teamed up with the elite Kyrie Irving and was looking to bring a title to his home city.

Lebron Wins A Title For Cleveland

After going down 3-1 to the Warriors, Lebron proved his greatness and led his team to come back and win the 2016 NBA Championship. This historical comeback put Lebron up there with the greatests who have ever touched a basketball.

Lebron Joins The Lakers

After a few years in Cleveland, Lebron decided to take his talents to Los Angeles in hopes of building a true superteam. He is currently teaming up with the elite Anthony Davis and hopes to bring one more elite player on board to complete the superteam.
